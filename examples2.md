# SPOILER ALERT!!!

## Notable Stories in Capcom Arcade Vampire Savior

## Gallon

Gallon gets to return home after winning against himself. Yet he finds that there lies within him the longing for more.

## Sasquatch

Sasquatch defeats Jedah whom he thought had imprisoned his kin. However, he learns that, on the contrary, his kin were treated kindly by Jedah.

## Bishamon

Bishamon gains ever more powerful upon defeating Jedah. However, a monk, who understands that his power lies in his demonic armor, seals it and frees the soul of the warrior that it had possessed.

## Victor

Victor gets to save little girl Emily with all his strength. When Emily wakes up, she finds Victor lifeless, but smiling.
